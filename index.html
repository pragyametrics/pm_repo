About Pragyametrics

At Pragyametrics, we empower Indian enterprises to harness the true potential of Artificial Intelligence through actionable strategy, expert implementation, and contextual innovation.

Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and real-world business needs, Pragyametrics focuses exclusively on the Indian market. We understand that successful AI adoption in India requires more than just technology—it demands cultural sensitivity, multilingual adaptability, cost-effective solutions, and industry-specific expertise.

We combine deep domain knowledge with hands-on technical skill to deliver AI solutions that are practical, scalable, and measurable. From AI readiness assessments to full-scale implementation support, our team ensures you are guided at every step—from idea to execution.

Our independent, vendor-neutral approach guarantees that our recommendations are aligned with your goals—not third-party partnerships. Whether you're just beginning your AI journey or scaling enterprise-wide adoption, Pragyametrics is your trusted partner for driving value through intelligent transformation.

Founder & CEO

Sudha Rani brings exceptional vision and technical depth to Pragyametrics as its Founder and CEO. Trained as a chemical engineer and later specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning with a Post Graduate Diploma from MIT World Peace University, Pune, she bridges the analytical discipline of engineering with the transformative potential of AI. Her ability to translate complex systems into scalable, intelligent solutions has delivered measurable outcomes for clients across diverse sectors in India.

A trailblazer in AI-driven innovation, Sudha has spearheaded the development of advanced systems such as predictive maintenance platforms and healthcare AI applications tailored to India's critical infrastructural needs. Notably, her creation of the integrated multi-cloud, multi-model PM-JAY RAG hospital system and intelligent bed availability search algorithms exemplifies her commitment to solving high-impact, real-world problems through data-driven automation and machine learning.

Her core strength lies in designing agentic AI models capable of autonomously executing complex operational workflows. This capability positions Pragyametrics at the forefront of delivering scalable, end-to-end digital transformation solutions. Guided by Sudha’s leadership, the company adheres to a vendor-neutral philosophy—prioritizing clients’ strategic goals and ensuring the integration of global best practices within India's unique and evolving market landscape.
